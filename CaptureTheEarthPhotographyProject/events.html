Latest News

Recently, fellow society members embarked on a journey to Iceland, known for its stunning landscapes and natural wonders. Equipped with their cameras, tripods, and lenses, they set out to capture the beauty of the country through their lenses. From the rugged coastline with its crashing waves to the majestic waterfalls and the otherworldly geothermal pools, Iceland proved to be a photographer's paradise. The group hiked through scenic trails and hiked across glaciers to capture the perfect shot. They also had the opportunity to witness the spectacular Northern Lights, a breathtaking display of nature's beauty. Their trip was not just about capturing images, but also learning from each other, exchanging tips, and bonding over their shared passion for photography. The photographs captured by the group of the breathtaking beauty of Iceland are now live in our gallery!
