History of the Society

Capture the Earth, a nature photography college society, was founded in 2010 by a group of photography students who shared a common love for capturing the beauty and wonder of the natural world.
The society's members come from diverse academic backgrounds and share a passion for exploring the outdoors and documenting their experiences through photography.
Since its founding, Capture the Earth has organized various photography workshops, field trips, and exhibitions to provide opportunities for its members to hone their skills, share their knowledge, and showcase their work.
The society also actively participates in various conservation initiatives and works closely with environmental organizations to promote the preservation of natural habitats and biodiversity.
Through its activities and events, Capture the Earth has become a vibrant and inclusive community of nature photographers on campus, inspiring its members to explore and appreciate the beauty of the natural world, while also promoting the importance of conservation and environmental stewardship.
